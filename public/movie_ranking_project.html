The Best Movies According to Tamiris

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Curious Case of Benjamin Button

The film The Curious Case of Benjamin Button reflects the fragility of life,
no matter where we start, but an end is certain for all of us, death

Face/Off

The film Face/Off is a breathtaking film from beginning to end and it's incredible
how it makes you root for the villain and the good guy at the same time.

The Lion King

The Lion King is the best children's film of all time, the story and the music are
simply moving, it is one of the films that my children will be forced to watch.
